Grand Theft Auto V is a 2013 action-adventure game developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games. It is the seventh main entry in the Grand Theft Auto series, following 2008's Grand Theft Auto IV, and the fifteenth instalment overall. Set within the fictional state of San Andreas, based on Southern California, the single-player story follows three protagonists—retired bank robber Michael De Santa (Ned Luke), street gangster Franklin Clinton (Shawn Fonteno), and drug dealer and gunrunner Trevor Philips (Steven Ogg), and their attempts to commit heists while under pressure from a corrupt government agency and powerful criminals. Players freely roam San Andreas's open world countryside and fictional city of Los Santos, based on Los Angeles.

Minecraft is a sandbox video game focused on creative freedom, exploration, and survival. It allows players to build structures, craft items, and explore diverse virtual worlds. Access to the game and its features is managed through Microsoft or Google Play accounts, enabling play across various devices. The game has a large online community centered around player-created content.

Among Us is an online multiplayer social deduction game where players are either Crewmates, who must complete tasks and identify the Impostors, or Impostors, who sabotage and eliminate the crew. The game relies heavily on communication and observation, with players holding meetings to discuss suspicions and vote to eject potential Impostors, leading to tense and deceptive gameplay that experienced a surge in popularity in 2020, and is playable across multiple platforms.

Fortnite is a free-to-play online video game known for its "Battle Royale" mode, where players fight to be the last one standing. It's a cultural phenomenon with frequent in-game events, collaborations with popular franchises, and a vibrant, cartoonish art style. Fortnite generates revenue through cosmetic item sales and has evolved into a social platform, featuring diverse game modes beyond Battle Royale such as racing, music, and lego style gameplay.
